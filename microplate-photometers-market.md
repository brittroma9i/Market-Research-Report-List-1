<p><strong>The Microplate Photometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microplate photometers market comprises two primary types: optical filter microplate readers and optical grating microplate readers. Optical filter microplate readers utilize specific filters to isolate wavelengths, providing high sensitivity and simplicity for various applications. In contrast, optical grating microplate readers employ a diffraction grating to disperse light, allowing for the measurement of multiple wavelengths simultaneously, enhancing versatility and throughput. Both types are crucial in various fields, including biomedical research and drug discovery, for precise and efficient sample analysis.</p></p>

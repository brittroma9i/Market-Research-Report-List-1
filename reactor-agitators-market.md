<p><p>The reactor agitators market includes various types based on gear design, each offering unique advantages. Bevel gear agitators allow for compact designs and are suitable for changing the direction of drive shafts, making them versatile in application. Helical gear agitators feature smooth power transfer and reduced noise, ideal for high-viscosity materials. Each type is essential for optimizing mixing processes in various industrial applications.</p></p>

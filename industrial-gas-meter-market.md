<p><strong>The Industrial Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Gas Meter</li><li>Turbine Gas Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial gas meter market primarily includes diaphragm gas meters and turbine gas meters. Diaphragm gas meters utilize flexible diaphragms to measure gas flow, making them ideal for low to medium flow rates in residential and commercial applications. In contrast, turbine gas meters measure higher flow rates by using a rotating turbine to gauge gas movement, making them suitable for industrial settings. Both types ensure accurate measurement and efficient billing, thereby supporting the optimal management of gas consumption across various applications.</p></p>

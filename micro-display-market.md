<p><ul><li>Reflective Displays</li><li>Transmissive Displays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro-display market consists primarily of two types: reflective displays and transmissive displays. Reflective displays utilize ambient light to create images, making them energy-efficient and suitable for well-lit environments, often used in head-mounted displays. In contrast, transmissive displays rely on backlight sources to project images, providing brighter visuals, ideal for applications like virtual reality and wearables in darker settings. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing user experience through compact and high-resolution visuals.</p></p>
